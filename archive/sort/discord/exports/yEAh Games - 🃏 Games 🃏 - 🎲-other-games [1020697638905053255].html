Once their Team has 3 points they win. B: If an Unfriendly Person is tagged they have to go to the Jail. If that person is holding the Item then they have to return the item to it's desiganted spot, the team loses a point, and then go to the Jail. JAIL: Jail is a place everyone agrees on to put Unfriendly People that have been tagged by a Jailer. If an Unfriendly Person (That has not been tagged) goes to the jail, every Unfriendly Person is able to leave the Jail. BASE: The base is a place where a team of Unfriendly People have chosen to need to bring the Item. This cannot be close by to the Designated Spot For Item. 3. More rules: Unfriendly People cannot tag other Unfriendly People if they have received the Item in the previous 3 seconds. UTENSILS Everyone starts with two fingers, one on each hand. Every player has 2 options for their turn: - Rearrange the organization of their fingers, or - Attack another player's hand If they rearrange the organization of their fingers, they can move fingers from one hand to another in any way they like. However, you cannot shift fingers such that the number of fingers on both hands are simply swapped and not changed. If they instead attack another player's hand, they must hit the player's hand with a hand of their own. The player increases their number of fingers by the amount of fingers on your chosen hand. Once a player has finished their turn, the next player does something. - You cannot have more than 4 fingers on one hand. If you end up with 5 or more, the entire hand is removed. - You cannot attack an empty hand. - You win by leaving your opponent(s) with no fingers left.
